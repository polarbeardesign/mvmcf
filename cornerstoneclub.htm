Cornerstone Club - Quarterly Giving

The foundation of every life we touch.

Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation’s definition of a Cornerstone Club member is one who supports the Foundation by making an annual commitment to contribute quarterly to insure a regular source of funds in support of the Medical Center’s mission.

One of the challenges to running a successful not-for-profit Medical Center is to ensure that there is adequate resources year round to support the operation to make sure that the highest-quality of health care is available in the community.

Through quarterly donations, Cornerstone Club members provide the Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation with a consistent and reliable source of funding that allows us to plan ahead for the needs of the Medical Center.

What is your gift equivalent to?

$50 - meals for patients for a day | $100 - telephone service for a day | $250 - Lab supplies for a day
$500 - CT scan services for a day | $750 - drugs for patients for a day | $1000 - medical supplies for two days or heating/cooling the Medical Center for three days.

Why should you join the Cornerstone Club?

There are no club meetings. You won't have to pass an initiation. There are no secret handshakes. You won't have to wear a funny hat. You won't have to join a committee.

  • You may enjoy hassle-free quarterly donations. 
  • You may manage automatic donations from your credit card.
  • Your donation is put to work immediately in the community.
  • Your donation is tax deductible.


Ready to join the Cornerstone Club?

Join online - contribute with a credit card online - Please check Recurring Donation in the Frequency Section, in the notes section please indicate your Cornerstone Club Annual Pledge. See the Donate Now page, and click the Donate Now button.
