Larry Zabel Prints.... MVMC has eight beautifully framed, Larry Zabel prints for sale. Youcan see the prints hanging on the walls at the MedicalCenter. Six of the fourteen prints donated have been sold. Funds for these prints will go directly to the MVMC, a portion of your purchase is tax deductible. For more information call the foundation at (406) 682-6641 or 6630.

These prints were donated by the LeSuer Family, framing was donated by Right Angles in Ennis. (Larry Zabel himself donated the above beautiful painting "Fly Fishing on the Madison", it is in the entry way of the hospital and is NOT for sale).

North Side Art Gallery.... MVMC is proud to present an Art Gallery Wall, with changing displays of art for sale. The sales will benefit MVMC as well as the artist.

Please stop by the Medical Center's north entrance and view the display. For more information please call, MVMC Art Coordinator Donna Evans at (406) 599-1668.

Types of Gifts Commonly Received

The Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation values the friendship of our supporters and feels it is important to express our appreciation. Unless we are directed otherwise, contributions received from individuals, businesses, service clubs and community organizations will be recognized. We have plans for a donor recognition wall and naming recognition plaques.

Memorial Giving

To request that memorials be made in the name of a loved one, please include the following in the obituary notice: " The family of ... requests that contributions be made to Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation, PO Box 993, Ennis, MT 59729." For more information please call (406) 682-6641.

Naming Recognition

Many supporters have donated to rooms or departments of the Madison Valley Medical Center. The foundation will recognize these donors with engraved plaques, and name the departments after those donors. There are still a few opportunities to give, for more information contact the foundation at (406) 682-6641.
