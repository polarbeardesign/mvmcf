Your Support is Key

The medical center has six board certified providers on staff. R.D. Marks, MD is now the cheif of medical staff. The other providers are; Dr. Curtis Blake, Dr. Patricia Moran, and PA-Cs Cary Wilson, Nancy Fitzpatrick, and Marty Hensel. You can support the medical center by making an appointment to have your medical needs met here in Ennis. Call the hospital at (406) 682-6862 or the clinic at (406) 682-4223.

Support is also needed to complete many aspects of the facility, such as; installing permanent signage outside and inside, completing a landscaping plan, purchasing furnishings for some areas and fulfilling department "wish lists" for various types of equipment.

We encourage the people of our community to stop in, see the new facility, meet the new providers and the caring staff. For more information on how you can help support the Madison Valley Medical Center, contact the Foundation Executive Director, Christie Trapp at (406) 682-6630 or 6641.

The Madison Valley Medical Center

The beautiful new facility is filled with State-of-the-Art Technology. Patients have the comfort of private rooms and private baths, most rooms also enjoy beautiful views of the valley and surrounding mountains.

The building is a two-level, 36,470 square foot medical facility with ten acute care hospital beds, a full laboratory, imaging department, emergency room and a nine exam room clinic.
