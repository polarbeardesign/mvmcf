Shop & Support
Larry Zabel Prints | North Side Gallery | Gewgaw Designs Jewelry

Our online store features gifts, art and more. A portion of the royalties from sales help carry out the Foundation’s mission to support the health and well-being of people in Madison County and adjacent communities in southwest Montana.

As an added thanks to our donors who provide these lovely items, we are including their contact information so you can learn more about their work and products. We hope that you will support their business and generosity to the Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation.

North Side Art Gallery

We are proud to present an Art Gallery Wall, with changing displays of art for sale. The proceeds from art sales will benefit The Medical Center as well as the artist.

Please stop by the Medical Center's north entrance to view the display. For more information please call, MVMC Art Coordinator Donna Evans at (406) 599-1668.

 

Gewgaw Designs




Inspired by Nature, Jewelry by Heidi Gildred. As a jewelry designer, I'm inspired by the beauty of the world we live in, working with treasures from the sea and natural stones created within the earth upon which we
exist. Every creation is unique and one of a kind.

I believe in giving back to my community so 10% of all sales will be donated to the new Madison Valley Medical Center in Ennis, Montana. Although my heart is in Montana (where I live in the summer), my business is based in California, however I will pay the sales tax for you if I ship to this state. Visit Gewgaw Design's website at http://www.gewgawdesigns.etsy.com.
