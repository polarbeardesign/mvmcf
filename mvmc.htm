Madison Valley Medical Center

The Madison Valley Medical Center is a two-level, 36,470 square foot medical facility with 10 acute care hospital beds, a full laboratory, imaging department, emergency room and a 9 exam room clinic.

Tom Mitchell recently took over the CEO position at the medical center. The Madison Valley Medical Center (MVMC) had been looking for a CEO since early 2009.

Mitchell who hails from Utah, works for a hospital management company based in Deer Lodge, Cypress Health Systems (CHS). CHS specializes in managing rural critical access hospitals and is the over arching management agency, Mitchell is their representative at the medical center. Tony Pfaff, owner of CHS has a long record of recruiting doctors to rural hospitals and Mitchell has been key to that success.

Tom Mitchell specializes in two things, the financial side of the hospital operations and recruiting doctors. Since he started in November 2009, Mitchell has added three doctors and a physicians assistant to the MVMC staff.
