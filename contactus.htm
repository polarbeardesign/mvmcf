Staff

Executive Director
Christie S. Trapp
Phone: (406) 682-6630
E-Mail: ctrapp@mvmcf.org

Christie joined the Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation in the Winter of 2010, bringing her 20 years of experience in nonprofit and business management to the organization.  Prior to joining the Foundation, Christie helped to build the impact and long-term sustainability of a Texas nonprofit, Habitat for Humanity, dedicated to low-income families, was active in the development of the 2030 Comprehensive Plan of her adopted community Georgetown, TX, and assisted global technology companies to communicate their value across U.S., Asian and European markets.  In so doing, Christie gained the ability to work with diverse constituencies and forge local and global partnerships to bring services to fruition.  With a background in biology, and a lifetime of appreciation of the Madison Valley, Christie is glad to be back home in Montana, andable to use her strategic planning, donor relations, media and organizational abilities to promote the interdependence of quality health care and improved health outcomes.
