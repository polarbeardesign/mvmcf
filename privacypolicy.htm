Your Privacy Matters


Information, including but not limited to name, address, phone number and email address, collected by the Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation will not be sold or distributed to any organization outside the Foundation without express written consent.

The Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation may from time-to-time, publish names and gift levels as a way of publicly thanking its donors. An individual donor may request that their gift not be published. Corporate gifts will be omitted from such lists if the Chief Executive Officer, or equivalent, requests that the gift be anonymous. Individuals will not be solicited without their express consent.

Upon provision of a street address or email address, you will receive periodic mail and/or email from the Madison Valley Medical Center Foundation unless you request no such mailings. To request that you be removed from mail and/or email lists you may either: write, call or email.
